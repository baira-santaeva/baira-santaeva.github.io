Pro Kitchen Tips

Small tricks that make a big difference at the stove.

🧂 Season in layers

Add salt at each stage of cooking rather than just at the end — it builds depth and brings out natural flavours.

🔪 Keep your knives sharp

A sharp knife is safer and more precise. Hone your blade before each session and sharpen it every few months.

🍳 Don't crowd the pan

Overcrowding drops the pan temperature and steams instead of sears. Cook in batches for a golden crust.

🌡️ Use a meat thermometer

Stop guessing doneness. A thermometer removes the guesswork and guarantees perfectly cooked protein every time.

🧄 Bloom your spices

Toast whole spices or fry ground ones in oil for 30 seconds before adding other ingredients to unlock their full aroma.

🫙 Mise en place

Prep and measure all ingredients before you start cooking. It keeps you calm, organised, and produces better results.
